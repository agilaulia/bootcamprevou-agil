# Quick Start Guide - To-Do Life Dashboard

## Getting Started

### Opening the Dashboard
1. Navigate to the project directory
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
   - Double-click the file, or
   - Right-click → Open with → Your preferred browser
3. The dashboard will load instantly (no server required)

## First Steps

### 1. Personalize Your Dashboard
- Go to the **Settings** section at the bottom
- Enter your name in the "Your Name" field
- Press Enter or click outside the field
- Your name will appear in the greeting at the top

### 2. Customize Your Timer
- In **Settings**, enter your preferred timer duration (1-120 minutes)
- Default is 25 minutes (standard Pomodoro)
- Press Enter to save
- The timer will update immediately

### 3. Add Your First Task
- In the **Tasks** section (right side)
- Type your task in the input field
- Press Enter or click "Add"
- Your task appears in the list below

### 4. Manage Tasks
- **Complete**: Click the checkbox to mark done (strikethrough)
- **Edit**: Double-click the task text or click "Edit"
- **Delete**: Click "Delete" button (confirmation required)

### 5. Add Quick Links
- In the **Quick Links** section (bottom)
- Enter a name (e.g., "Gmail")
- Enter a URL (e.g., "gmail.com" or "https://gmail.com")
- Click "Add Link"
- Click the link to open in a new tab

### 6. Use the Focus Timer
- In the **Focus Timer** section (left side)
- Click "Start" to begin countdown
- Click "Stop" to pause
- Click "Reset" to return to initial duration
- Timer automatically stops at 00:00

### 7. Switch Themes
- Click the "Toggle Theme" button in Settings
- Switch between Light and Dark mode
- Your preference is saved automatically

## Features Overview

### Top Section
- **Time Display**: Current time (updates every second)
- **Date Display**: Current date with day of week
- **Greeting**: Personalized time-based greeting

### Left Section - Focus Timer
- Pomodoro-style countdown timer
- Customizable duration
- Start/Stop/Reset controls

### Right Section - Task Manager
- Add, edit, delete tasks
- Mark tasks complete
- All tasks saved automatically

### Bottom Section - Quick Links
- Save favorite website shortcuts
- One-click access
- Manage your links easily

### Settings
- Configure your name
- Set timer duration
- Toggle light/dark theme

## Tips & Tricks

### Keyboard Shortcuts
- Press **Enter** in any input field to submit
- Press **Tab** to navigate between fields
- Press **Escape** when editing a task to cancel

### Data Persistence
- All your data is saved automatically in your browser
- Tasks, links, settings, and theme persist across sessions
- Data is stored locally (no internet required)

### Productivity Tips
1. Start your day by reviewing your tasks
2. Use the Pomodoro timer for focused work sessions
3. Add frequently used websites to Quick Links
4. Switch to Dark mode for evening work
5. Keep task descriptions clear and concise

## Troubleshooting

### Data Not Saving?
- Check if browser is in private/incognito mode
- Private browsing may prevent data persistence
- Use normal browsing mode for data to persist

### Timer Not Working?
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
- Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)

### Links Not Opening?
- Check if pop-up blocker is enabled
- Allow pop-ups for this page
- Links open in new tabs
